IN-APP PURCHASES — HOW TO TRIGGER THE PAYWALL
----------------------------------------------
The app has a first-session guardrail: the paywall does NOT appear on the
very first launch (by design, to protect new-user retention).

To reach the paywall after first launch:

  Option A — Settings tab (always accessible, no guardrail):
    1. Open the app.
    2. Tap the "Ajustes" (Settings) tab at the bottom.
    3. Tap "Ver planos" in the subscription card.
    4. The paywall appears immediately.

  Option B — Feature block (after first session):
    1. Open the app and complete one daily check-in.
    2. Navigate to Histórico or Favoritos.
    3. Tap any locked item — the premium block sheet appears.
    4. Tap "Ver planos" to open the full paywall.

ADDITIONAL NOTES
----------------
• The app is a daily stoic practice companion: each day the user receives a
  curated quote + practical recommendation + check-in.
• Free users have full access to the daily ritual. Pro unlocks history,
  favorites, and guided tracks.
• Push notifications are used only for the daily ritual reminder. Permission
  is requested contextually, not on first launch.
• No user-generated content. No third-party logins other than Apple and Google.
• Crash reporting and analytics are handled via Firebase (no PII stored).
